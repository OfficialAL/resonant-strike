## Control Philosophy

### Traditional Wing Chun Principles
- **Centerline Theory:** All techniques protect and attack along the body's centerline
- **Economy of Motion:** Shortest distance between two points
- **Simultaneous Attack and Defense:** Tan Da embodies this principle
- **Sensitivity:** Chi Sau develops tactile awareness

### Game Implementation
- **Stance First:** Choose your defensive position with W/A/S/D
- **Technique Second:** Execute your attack with I/J/K/L
- **Energy Follows:** Visual energy waves show internal power flow
- **Range Matters:** Close-quarters combat honors Wing Chun distance

## Combat Flow

### Basic Sequence
1. **Assess Enemy:** Watch for attack telegraphs
2. **Choose Stance:** Select appropriate Wing Chun stance (W/A/S/D)
3. **Execute Technique:** Perform Wing Chun technique (I/J/K/L)

### Advanced Combinations

#### Defensive Combinations
- **Wu Sau + Pak Sau (S + L):** Protect centerline then create opening
- **Tan Sau + Tan Da (A + J):** Redirect force then counter-attack

- **Bong Sau + Tan Da (W + J):** Wing deflection into simultaneous counter
- **Wu Sau + Chain Punch (S + I):** Centerline protection into power strike
